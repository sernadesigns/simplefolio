About me


I'm a professional Front End Software Engineer and passionate about building great sites and tools and continually learning and refining my skills.

I have helped my clients create their first websites, coaching them through the entire process, from defining the website's purpose, laying out a site architecture, storyboarding, designing, and developing a responsive, mobile-first site, to give their first online presence a solid boost.

Lately, I have shifted from developing websites to developing web applications that are scalable and composed of reusable components, primarily in Ember.js and Node.js, although I am also continually exploring other front end frameworks and solutions.

When I am not writing code or exploring new things in development, I love to spend time with my wife and five kids. We enjoy anything outdoors, theme parks, and watching Dodgers baseball.
